Math methods

    .random()
        Generates a random decimal number between 0 (inclusive) and 1 (exclusive).

    .round()
        Rounds the number up or down to its nearest whole number.

    .ceil()
        Rounds the number up to its nearest whole number.

    .floor()
        Rounds the number down to its nearest whole number.

    .toFixed(<number of decimals>)
        Rounds the number to the specified number of decimals.

    .max(<arrange of numbers>)
        Computes the maximum value in an arrange of comma-separated values.
