INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

* Description
  Website to manage inventory of a store. Great for companies, stores or people who are managing their inventory using spreadsheets or other ancient and non-scalable technologies.

* Attributes and recommendations
  > Basic styling, no need to emphasize on design.
  > Great emphasis on backend. Database and system architecture design is fundamental.
  > Emphasis on ease-of-use. The user needs to fill data seamlessly.
  > Fast loading times, very low resource demanding.

* Maximum completion time
  34 days (thorough system and database design, whole days on meetings, one extra functionality + web hosting)
